2025-05-07 - Home - Exhaustion

I am very tired brothers.

Every day persists to

blend into each other.

Each day repeating the same pattern.

Job unchanging,

unfulfilled on most fronts.

Such a place

puts a man into a downward spiral

that keeps him slave

to his circumstances.


Exhaustion of ones life

drains the motivation necessarry

to change it.

Lack of discipline, as such,

which is the case

for most young men these days,

your writer included,

locks the chains

on his neck,

and his hands,

and his legs.


When you need the discipline

you are to unmotivated to form it.


When you don't need it,

you believe you never will.


This was always the case,

and it was strong will

and hardship

that helped men without discipline build it.


But those things are not self-evident

until it is too late.

As such a primary role of the father

was to build such dicipline

into their boys from their youth.


Life was never more full

of engaging things than now,

yet boredom is more prevelant now,

than ever in history.


This is due to

that lack of discipline,

for it was that,

in fact,

which kept boredom at bay.


And boredom, lack of motivation,

habits, and discipline,

those lead to depression.


Boomers may have destroyed

the structures of society,

but it was Gen X fathers who

destroyed the individuals who

will make the society.


Thot daughter, or

jobless loveless son?

That's all there is left.

Surely they will re-appear some day...

I tell you when they will re-apper. When I buy new ones. So then I will have 5 or more in my house. If I bought new ones today, they will appear as soon as the postman knocks on the door. If I didn't buy them today, they will never re-appear.
